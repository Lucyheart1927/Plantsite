Weekly Calendar

Week 1: Germination

Your seeds are sprouting!

Weekly Tasks

  • Keep soil moist (not soggy)
  • Keep temperature around 65–75°F
  • Use gentle, indirect sunlight

Week 2: First Leaves

True leaves begin to form.

Weekly Tasks

  • Give a bit more light each day
  • Water when the top soil dries
  • Rotate pots to keep growth straight

Week 3: Vegetative Growth

More leaves and stronger stems.

Weekly Tasks

  • Start a light fertilizer schedule
  • Give 6–8 hours of sunlight
  • Check leaves for pests

Week 4: Getting Ready to Flower

Plants are almost ready to bloom.

Weekly Tasks

  • Support stems if leaning
  • Keep watering consistent
  • Remove extra side shoots
